White Wine Ragu with Carrots

Ingredients
  • 2.5 C Brunoise-Cut Carrots (8-10 Carrots)
  • 6 Garlic Cloves Minced
  • Thyme (Fresh)
  • 1.5 C Parmesan (Grated)
  • 1 lb Italian Sausage w/ Casings Removed (4-5 Sausages)
  • 2 T Unsalted Butter
  • 1 t Red Pepper Flakes
  • 0.5 C Dry White Wine (Chardonnay)
  • 0.5 C Water
  • 12 oz Orecchiette Pasta (1 Box)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Pour a glug of Olive Oil in a heated large sauce pan on medium-high.
  2. Pour in the Carrots and Garlic reducing the heat to medium-low once sizzling. Combine 1 t of Salt and continuously stir and fold the vegetables for 10 minutes or until softened. Do not let them brown.
  3. Combine Italian Sausage and cook thoroughly for 10 minutes while breaking large chunks of meat.
  4. Add the Pepper Flakes and generous sprigs of Thyme to the mixture then pour in the Wine and Water. Lower heat to a gentle simmer for 35 - 40 minutes. Add a little more water if the sauce begins to dry.
  5. While the ragu is cooking, bring a large pot of heavily salted water to a boil. Cook the Pasta based on box instructions.
  6. In the meantime, remove Thyme leaves from the stems for garnish. Measure the desired quantity with your heart.
  7. Once the ragu is cooked, stir in the Butter and 1 C of Parmesan until melted. Reserve the remainder of the cheese for garnish.
  8. When the pasta is cooked, reserve about 1 C of pasta water. Drain the pasta and combine with the ragu.
  9. Using your judgment, pour in as much reserved pasta water as needed to moisten the ragu, avoiding a watery mixture.
  10. Season with Salt and Pepper to taste. Garnish with Thyme, Parmesan Cheese, and/or Pepper Flakes to preferrence.
